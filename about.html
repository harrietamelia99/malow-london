About us

MALOW London started with a very real problem: loving heels, but never being able to wear them for as long as you’d like.

Our co-founder, Emily Bennett, has spent over 20 years designing footwear, and like so many women, she always found herself reaching for beautiful heels… only to regret it a few hours later. Whether it was a wedding, night out or a day at the races, the same thing kept happening: amazing shoes, but aching feet.

MALOW London was created to bring together style and comfort in a way that actually works. Shoes you can wear without counting down the minutes until you can take them off.

Every pair is designed to feel as good as it looks. Think timeless shapes, modern touches, and colours that go with everything in your wardrobe. They’re made to be versatile, so you can wear them again and again, and still feel just as confident every time.

At the heart of it all, MALOW London is about enjoying the moment: staying comfortable, feeling glamorous, and never having to choose between the two.
