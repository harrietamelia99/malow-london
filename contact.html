Frequently asked questions

How long will my order take to arrive?

We ship with Evri. Estimated transit after dispatch: UK 1–3 working days; Northern Ireland 3–5; EU 5–8; US 7–10. See our shipping page for full details.

Can I return or exchange my shoes?

Yes. We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided items are unworn and in original packaging. Exchange requests are processed within 3–5 working days of us receiving your return. Visit our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

I'm not sure about my size - what should I do?

Our styles run true to size. You can find our full UK, EU, and US conversion chart on the Size Guide page.

Where is my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. If you haven't received this within 24 hours of placing your order, please get in touch and include your order number.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes—we ship worldwide. Standard rates by region are on our shipping page; final shipping costs also appear at checkout. Customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Can I change or cancel my order?

We process orders quickly, but if you need to make a change, please email us at support@malowlondon.com as soon as possible with your order number. We'll do our best to help before dispatch.
