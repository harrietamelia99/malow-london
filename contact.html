Frequently asked questions

How long will my order take to arrive?

Standard UK delivery takes 3–5 working days. Express delivery (1–2 working days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 working days. See our full shipping page for details.

Can I return or exchange my shoes?

Yes. We accept returns within 30 days of delivery, provided items are unworn and in original packaging. Exchange requests are processed within 3–5 working days of us receiving your return. Visit our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

I'm not sure about my size - what should I do?

Our styles run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up. You can find our full conversion chart and foot measuring guide on the Size Guide page.

Where is my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. If you haven't received this within 24 hours of placing your order, please get in touch and include your order number.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes - we ship worldwide. International shipping rates and estimated delivery times are shown at checkout. Please note that customs duties and taxes may apply depending on your country and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Can I change or cancel my order?

We process orders quickly, but if you need to make a change, please email us at hello@malowlondon.com as soon as possible with your order number. We'll do our best to help before dispatch.
