Cookies Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on this website, and how you can control them.

Last updated: March 2026. See also our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work efficiently, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the site. Similar technologies include pixels and local storage.

How we use cookies

MALOW London uses cookies to operate this site (for example, keeping your session working), to remember choices such as language or region where applicable, and to measure site performance and marketing effectiveness where you have consented or the law allows.

Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary — required for core functionality (e.g. security, load balancing). These cannot usually be turned off without breaking the site.
  • Functional — remember your preferences and improve your experience.
  • Analytics / performance — help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g. pages viewed, errors) so we can improve it.
  • Marketing / advertising — used to deliver relevant ads or measure campaigns, where permitted.

When this site is connected to Shopify or other platforms, those providers may set their own cookies according to their policies and your choices in their cookie banner or preference centre.

Your choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings (block, delete, or alert you about cookies). You may also see a cookie banner on our site allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works.

For more information on managing cookies in common browsers, see your browser’s help section or visit aboutcookies.org.

Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy when our practices or the law change. Please check this page periodically; the “Last updated” date shows when it was last revised.
