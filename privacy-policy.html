Your data, your rights

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect when you shop or browse with MALOW London, how we use it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Last updated: March 2026. This page will be updated when formal contact and data-request channels are published.

Who we are

MALOW London (“we”, “us”, “our”) is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with this website and our products. We are based in the United Kingdom. This policy applies to information collected through malowlondon.com (and any successor site), email, and customer care channels.

Information we collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Identity and contact details — name, delivery and billing address, email address, and phone number when you place an order, create an account, or contact us.
  • Transaction data — items purchased, order history, payment status, and communications about your order. Payment card details are processed by our payment providers; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Technical and usage data — IP address, browser type, device information, approximate location, pages viewed, and referring URLs, collected via cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookies Policy).
  • Marketing preferences — whether you have signed up to our newsletter or opted in or out of marketing messages.

How we use your information

We use personal data to fulfil your orders, process payments, arrange delivery, handle returns, respond to enquiries, improve our website and products, comply with legal obligations, and (where you have consented or the law allows) send you news and offers about MALOW London.

Legal bases (UK GDPR)

Depending on the activity, we rely on: performance of a contract (e.g. processing your order); legitimate interests (e.g. fraud prevention, analytics, improving our services), balanced against your rights; legal obligation (e.g. tax and accounting records); and consent (e.g. certain marketing emails or non-essential cookies), where required.

Sharing your data

We share data with trusted service providers who work on our behalf, such as payment processors, delivery couriers, email platforms, and website hosting or e-commerce infrastructure (e.g. Shopify, when you migrate this site). They may only use your data for the purposes we specify and must protect it appropriately. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our customers.

International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the UK. Where we transfer personal data to countries that are not subject to an adequacy decision, we use appropriate safeguards such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or Addendum, or other approved mechanisms.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, including to meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Order and tax records may be retained for a number of years as required by law; marketing data is kept until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law you may have the right to: access, correct, or erase your personal data; restrict or object to certain processing; data portability; and withdraw consent where processing is consent-based. You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk. To exercise your rights, use the contact or account tools made available on this website when they are live.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure; we encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account details confidential.

Children

Our website and products are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have done so, please notify us through the contact options published on this site when available, and we will delete it promptly.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy where permitted by law.
