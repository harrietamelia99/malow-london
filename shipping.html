Shipping & Returns

Free UK standard delivery on all orders. Easy 30-day returns.

Delivery options

Standard UK Delivery Free

3–5 working days

Complimentary on every order. Delivered by Royal Mail Tracked 48. You'll receive a tracking link by email once dispatched.

Express UK Delivery £6.95

1–2 working days

Need it sooner? Choose Express at checkout for next-day or two-day delivery, dispatched via Royal Mail Special Delivery.

International Delivery From £12.95

7–14 working days

We ship worldwide. Exact rates are calculated at checkout based on your country. Tracking is included on all international orders.

Orders placed before 1pm GMT Monday–Friday are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after this time or at weekends are dispatched the next working day.

Returns & exchanges

01

Check eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be unworn, in original condition, and returned in their original box and packaging. We're unable to accept returns on items showing signs of wear.

02

Request a return

Email returns@malowlondon.com with your order number and reason for return. Our team will send you a prepaid Royal Mail returns label within 1 working day.

03

Send it back

Pack your shoes securely and drop the parcel at any Royal Mail post box or post office. Keep your proof of postage - we recommend using the tracked label provided.

04

Refund or exchange

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund or exchange within 3–5 working days. Refunds are issued to your original payment method. Exchanges are dispatched with complimentary standard delivery.

International customers are responsible for return postage costs unless the item is faulty or incorrect. We recommend using a tracked service, as we cannot accept responsibility for parcels lost in transit.

Faulty or incorrect items

We take quality seriously. If you've received a faulty or incorrect item, please contact us immediately at hello@malowlondon.com with your order number and a photo of the issue. We'll arrange a free return and replacement (or full refund) as a priority.
