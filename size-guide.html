Size Guide

All MALOW styles are available in UK sizes 3–8 and run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

Size conversions are approximate and may vary slightly between styles. When in doubt, size up - our memory foam footbeds adapt to your foot over time.

How to measure your foot

1

Prepare

Measure your feet in the afternoon - feet naturally swell slightly throughout the day, so an afternoon measurement gives the most accurate result. Wear the type of hosiery you plan to wear with the shoes.

2

Trace your foot

Place a sheet of plain paper on a hard floor. Stand on it with your weight evenly distributed and trace around your foot with a pencil held vertically - keep the pencil as close to your foot as possible.

3

Measure the length

Using a ruler, measure the distance from the back of the heel to the tip of the longest toe. This is your foot length in centimetres. Repeat for both feet and use the longer measurement.

4

Find your size

Match your measurement to the table above. If your measurement falls between two sizes, choose the larger size for the most comfortable fit - especially important for styles with a pointed toe.
